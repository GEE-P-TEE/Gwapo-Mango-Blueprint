# Gwapo Mango Tours - Master Plan
## Guimaras Mango Farm Experience Landing Page

### App Overview and Objectives
Gwapo Mango Tours is a premium tourism landing page designed to showcase and sell authentic mango farm experiences in Guimaras, Philippines. The platform connects Filipino and international tourists with world-famous Guimaras mangoes through guided farm tours, creating memorable culinary and cultural experiences.

**Mission**: To provide tourists with an authentic, premium mango-tasting adventure while supporting local Guimaras farmers and showcasing the island's agricultural heritage.

### Target Audience
- **Primary**: Filipino tourists from other provinces visiting Guimaras
- **Secondary**: International tourists exploring the Philippines
- **Tertiary**: Day-trippers from nearby Iloilo City
- **Niche**: Food enthusiasts and mango lovers seeking authentic experiences

### Core Features and Functionality
1. **Hero Landing Page** with compelling mango farm imagery and clear booking CTA
2. **Real-time Booking System** with 12-person daily capacity management
3. **Interactive Tour Itinerary** showcasing 4 premium mango farms
4. **Multi-payment Gateway** (Stripe, PayMaya, GCash)
5. **Instant Booking Confirmation** with pickup details
6. **Contact Integration** (WhatsApp, phone, email)
7. **Mobile-first Responsive Design** optimized for tourists on-the-go

### High-level Technical Stack Recommendations
- **Frontend**: Next.js 14 with TypeScript
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS + shadcn/UI components
- **Backend & Database**: Supabase (PostgreSQL)
- **Payment Processing**: Stripe API, PayMaya SDK, GCash integration
- **Calendar System**: Custom booking calendar with real-time availability
- **Authentication**: Simple booking form (no user accounts required)
- **Hosting**: Vercel (seamless Next.js deployment)
- **Maps**: Google Maps API for pickup location

# Gwapo Mango Tours - App Flow, Pages & Roles
## Site Structure and User Journey Mapping

### Core Pages Structure

#### 1. Homepage (`/`)
**Purpose**: Primary landing page to capture interest and drive bookings
**Key Elements**:
- Hero section with stunning mango farm imagery
- Clear value proposition: "Experience Guimaras' World-Famous Mangoes"
- Prominent CTA: "Book Your Mango Adventure Today!"
- Tour highlights preview (4 farms, traditional lunch, pickup included)
- Price display: ₱3,500 per person
- Quick booking widget showing next available dates
- Customer testimonials section
- Instagram feed integration

**Mobile Layout**: Single column, hero image, stacked content
**Desktop Layout**: Hero with overlay booking widget, two-column highlights

#### 2. About Us (`/about`)
**Purpose**: Build trust and showcase local expertise
**Key Elements**:
- Story of Gwapo Mango Tours and passion for Guimaras mangoes
- Meet the team section with local guide profiles
- Why choose us (local expertise, small groups, premium farms)
- Partnership with local farmers
- Commitment to sustainable tourism
- Awards or certifications (if any)

#### 3. Tour Details & Itinerary (`/tour-details`)
**Purpose**: Comprehensive tour information to convert interest to booking
**Key Elements**:
- **Detailed Timeline**:
  - 7:00 AM - Pickup at Jordan Ferry Dock
  - 8:00 AM - Oro Verde Mango Plantation (premium tasting)
  - 10:00 AM - Guimaras Wonder's Farm (tour + photos)
  - 12:00 PM - Traditional Filipino lunch with mango dishes
  - 2:00 PM - National Mango Research Center (educational)
  - 3:30 PM - Eli Sustituido Farm (final tasting + shopping)
  - 5:00 PM - Return to Jordan Ferry Dock
- **What's Included**: Transportation, entries, lunch, guides, tastings
- **What to Bring**: Comfortable shoes, camera, sun protection
- **Group Size**: Maximum 12 people for intimate experience
- **Booking CTA**: Prominent throughout page

#### 4. Booking System (`/book`)
**Purpose**: Convert visitors to paying customers
**Key Components**:
- **Step 1**: Date Selection
  - Interactive calendar showing availability
  - Real-time availability (12-person limit)

  - Payment method selection (Stripe, PayMaya, GCash)

  - Order summary with total (₱3,500)
  - Terms and conditions checkbox
- **Step 4**: Confirmation
  - Booking confirmation number

- Booking confirmation details

### User Roles and Access Rights

#### Tourist/Customer (Public Access)
**Permissions**:
- View all public pages
- Make bookings and payments
- Access booking confirmation page
- Contact tour operators
- View availability calendar

**Restrictions**:
- Cannot modify existing bookings (must contact directly)
- Cannot access other customers' information
- Cannot view admin dashboard

#### Tour Operator/Admin (Protected Access)
**Permissions**:
- View booking dashboard
- Manage tour availability
- Process refunds/cancellations
- View customer information
- Send confirmation emails
- Update tour details and pricing
- View payment reports
- Manage content updates

**Access Method**: Simple admin login (email/password)
**Dashboard Location**: `/admin` (password protected)
